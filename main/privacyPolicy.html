Privacy Policy for ECHO

Effective Date: January 2025

Introduction

ECHO ("we," "us," or "our") is a music streaming web app that allows users to stream music online for free. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

By using ECHO ("the Service"), you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: When you sign up for an account, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and any other details you provide voluntarily during the registration process.
  • Usage Data: We collect information on how you interact with our service, including the songs you stream, your search queries, device information (browser type, operating system), and usage patterns.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our website and to improve our services. You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To Provide the Service: We use the data to allow you to stream music and to provide you with a personalized experience.
  • To Improve the Service: We analyze usage data to improve the content and functionality of the Service, as well as to enhance user experience.
  • To Communicate with You: We may use your contact information to send you updates, promotional materials, or customer support-related messages.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who help us operate the Service, such as hosting services, email services, and analytics providers.
  • For Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, such as in response to a court order or subpoena.
  • With Your Consent: We may share your information with your consent, such as when you connect your account with third-party services.

4. Security of Your Information

We take the security of your personal information seriously and use commercially reasonable methods to protect it. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and Correction: You can access and update your account information at any time by logging into your account.
  • Opt-out of Communications: You can opt out of receiving promotional emails or communications by following the unsubscribe instructions in the email.
  • Cookies Management: You can control cookies through your browser settings. You can set your browser to refuse all cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under age 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page, and the "Effective Date" at the top of the page will be updated accordingly. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.
